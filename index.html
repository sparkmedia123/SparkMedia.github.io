Why SPARK MEDIA?
We named our company SPARK MEDIA? because spark means light and light sometimes mean positivity. Media is from social media, we put media to also spread positivity on social media by sharing recipes and teaching how to cook for other people.

Why Light?
Because if we help people, it lightens our heart by seeing the happy faces of the people who we helped. This media brand focuses on creating visually captivating content, showcasing innovative cooking techniques, and sparking creativity in the kitchen. The term "Spark" symbolizes the idea of setting ideas, flavors, and passion ablaze; making cooking an engaging and delightful experience for the audience.
